How to Play Splendor

Goal: Be the first to reach 15 prestige points.

On your turn you must do exactly one action:

  • Take 3 different tokens — one of each color (each must have ≥1 in the bank)
  • Take 2 same tokens — only if that color has 4+ in the bank
  • Reserve a card — from the table or from a deck (face-down); you get 1 gold if available. Max 3 reserved.
  • Buy a card — from the table or your reserved cards. Pay the token cost; cards give permanent gem bonuses for future buys.

Nobles visit you when you have enough gem bonuses to meet their requirements. Each noble is worth 3 prestige points. Only one noble per turn.

You cannot hold more than 10 tokens at the end of your turn. When the first player reaches the target, the round finishes so everyone has equal turns. Highest prestige wins.
